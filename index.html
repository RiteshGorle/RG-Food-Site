A LITTLE FLASHBACK

From the lands of Maratha to the homes of Bengalureans, Donne Biryani has won the hearts of desi lovers. The history of this landmark biryani dates back to the 17th century when military camps in the cantonment area of Bengaluru made Donne Biryani to feed large troops of the Maratha army. Traditionally ,donne birayni is slow-cooked in huge copper vessels over hot ccoals and mounds of this delicious ,piping hot rice is served in a leaf container called ‘donne’-a bowl made of dried areca nut plam leaves. Now,Desi Donne Biryani goes an extra mile to satisy food lovers from all corners of the world with its unique flavours!

What is Desi Donne?

Desi Donne Biryani is ancient and authentic. We always go the extra mile to satisfy food lovers from
all corners of the world. And so, we have taken our classic donne biryani and experimented with
Telangana and Andhra flavours for a regional touch and a few modern
flavours to give it a modern and contemporary twist all the while carefully retaining the authentic taste.Having said about the
authenticity in taste, we source our ingredients grown with love and compassion directly from the
smallholder farmers in the far off villages creating a meaningful impact for all.
